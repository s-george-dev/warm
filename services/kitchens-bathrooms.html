Kitchen & Bathroom FAQs

Do you install complete kitchens?

No, we don’t fit kitchen units or worktops. We handle all plumbing and heating connections — working alongside your chosen installer to ensure everything fits and functions perfectly.

What parts of a bathroom installation do you cover?

We install all bathroom appliances, pipework, radiators, towel rails, and waste connections. We leave the tiling, decorating, and finishing touches to your chosen specialists.

Can you coordinate with my builder or tiler?

Absolutely. We regularly liaise with other trades to align schedules and prevent delays. Clear communication ensures your project runs smoothly from start to finish.

Do you check water pressure and heating performance?

Yes — every job includes checks on flow rate, pressure, and heating balance. If issues arise, we’ll advise whether a repair or system upgrade is more cost-effective.

Can I upgrade my boiler at the same time?

Yes. Many customers use kitchen or bathroom refurbishments as an opportunity to modernise their boiler or heating system. Visit our Boiler Installation page to learn more.
