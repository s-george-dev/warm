Here for you

When your heating or hot water fails, you need more than just a quick visit — you need an engineer who can find the fault, fix it safely, and get everything running again. That’s exactly what we do.
At Warm Right, our engineers are Gas Safe and OFTEC registered, trained to diagnose and repair faults across Gas, LPG and Oil systems. We understand that breakdowns never happen at a convenient time, so we work efficiently, communicate clearly, and aim to restore warmth as soon as possible.

Fast, Reliable Response

Our scheduling team prioritises breakdown calls to make sure urgent cases are attended quickly. We carry a wide range of commonly used parts in our vans, which means many repairs can be completed on the first visit. If a part does need to be ordered, our suppliers provide a same-day or next-day delivery service, so you’re never left waiting unnecessarily.
We’ll keep you updated throughout, so you know exactly what’s happening.

Thorough Diagnostics

Every repair begins with a structured diagnostic process:

  • Initial assessment— we discuss symptoms, check system pressure, and inspect the appliance safely.
  • Testing & verification — using calibrated instruments to test electrical and combustion components.
  • Root-cause analysis — we don’t just swap parts; we identify why the fault occurred to prevent future issues.

Our engineers are also available for independent second opinions if you’ve been advised to replace a boiler and want an expert review before committing.

First-Time Fixes

Because our vehicles are stocked with high-turnover components and universal fittings, we achieve a high first-time fix rate.
Every repair is tested for safe operation and compliance with the latest Gas Safe and Building Regulation standards before we leave site.
