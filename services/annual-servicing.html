Why Annual Servicing Matters

A boiler service isn’t just a box to tick — it’s the best way to keep your system running efficiently, safely, and within warranty. Annual servicing helps prevent costly breakdowns, improves energy performance, and ensures your boiler remains compliant with manufacturer and insurance requirements.

What’s Included in a Warm Right Service

Every service is carried out by a qualified engineer who follows a detailed, manufacturer-approved checklist:

  • Safety checks — Gas pressure, flue integrity, and carbon monoxide levels are tested for safe operation.
  • Cleaning and inspection — Burners, heat exchangers, and condensate traps are cleaned and inspected.
  • Performance testing — We assess combustion efficiency and fine-tune settings to optimise fuel use.
  • System health — Expansion vessels, pumps, and controls are checked for correct operation.
  • Service record — You’ll receive an updated service certificate for your records and warranty.

For Homeowners

An annual boiler service is the simplest way to avoid unexpected breakdowns during winter. It also ensures your boiler continues running at peak efficiency, saving you money on energy bills. We’ll remind you when your next service is due, so you never miss a year.

For Landlords

Our engineers provide annual Gas Safety (CP12) checks alongside servicing, helping you meet legal requirements and protect your tenants. We can service multiple properties and provide digital records for easy compliance management.

We’re not just gas experts — we also service oil and LPG systems. Our OFTEC engineers use manufacturer tools and parts to ensure your oil-fired appliance runs cleanly and efficiently, reducing emissions and fuel consumption.
