Stay Compliant. Stay Covered.

Every landlord in the UK is legally required to ensure that gas appliances, fittings, and flues within their rental properties are safe for tenants to use. This is verified through a Gas Safety Certificate (CP12), which must be renewed annually by a qualified Gas Safe engineer. At Warm Right, we make the process simple — from booking to certification.

What’s Included in a Landlord Gas Safety Check?

  • Full appliance inspection — boilers, cookers, fires, and any other gas appliances tested for safe operation.
  • Flue and ventilation check — ensuring combustion gases are expelled safely and air supply is adequate.
  • Gas tightness test — detecting any leaks or pressure drops within the property’s system.
  • Certificate issued on completion — detailed digital CP12 report emailed directly for your records.

Your Legal Responsibilities

The Gas Safety (Installation and Use) Regulations 1998 require landlords to:

  • Ensure all gas appliances, fittings, and flues are maintained in a safe condition.
  • Have a Gas Safe registered engineer carry out annual safety checks.
  • Provide tenants with a copy of the CP12 certificate within 28 days of the inspection.
  • Keep records for at least two years.

Non-compliance can lead to fines and invalid insurance, so timely inspections are essential.
