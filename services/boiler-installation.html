Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I need a new boiler?

If your boiler is breaking down frequently, making unusual noises, or no longer heating efficiently, it may be time to consider a replacement. However, many issues can still be repaired — our Second Opinion Service can confirm whether a new boiler is genuinely necessary.

Can a boiler really be “beyond repair”?

Sometimes engineers declare a boiler obsolete when certain parts are harder to source. WarmRight engineers have access to a wide supplier network — and we often find genuine parts that others claim are unavailable. It’s worth checking before replacing.

How long does installation take?

Most standard installations are completed in 1–2 days. More complex systems, such as conversions or unvented cylinder installations, may take a little longer. We’ll always give you a clear timeline upfront.

Will I get a warranty?

Yes — all new boilers come with full manufacturer warranties, and we register them for you. We also offer ongoing servicing to protect your warranty and keep your system running efficiently. See our Annual Servicing page for details.

What areas do you cover?

We install boilers across Kent, South East London, and East Sussex. If you’re unsure whether we cover your area, view our area coverage or get in touch — we’ll be happy to confirm.
