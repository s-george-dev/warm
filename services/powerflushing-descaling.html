Over time, central heating systems collect a build-up of rust, sludge, and scale that can clog radiators and strain your boiler. A powerflush or descale treatment restores water flow, improves heating performance, and extends the life of your system. Warm Right engineers use industry-leading equipment to deep-clean your system safely and efficiently.

What is Powerflushing?

Powerflushing is a process that circulates a specialist cleaning solution at high velocity through your heating system using a dedicated powerflush machine. It dislodges and removes debris, rust, and magnetite sludge that build up over time — restoring your system to optimal performance. Once complete, we add an inhibitor chemical to prevent future corrosion.

Signs You May Need a Powerflush

  • Cold spots on radiators or radiators slow to heat up.
  • Noisy boiler or pump.
  • Discoloured or dirty system water.
  • Frequent boiler lockouts or poor circulation.
  • Radiators needing frequent bleeding.
  • Uneven heat distribution across your home.

Descaling for Hard Water Systems

In hard water areas, limescale build-up can block heat exchangers and reduce efficiency. Our descaling service safely removes limescale deposits from boilers and system pipework using a controlled chemical flush. This helps restore full performance and reduces the strain on pumps, valves, and your boiler.

Aftercare & Maintenance

Once your system has been powerflushed or descaled, we can fit a magnetic filter to capture any remaining particles and prevent future build-up. We also recommend regular boiler servicing to keep your system healthy and maintain your manufacturer’s warranty.
