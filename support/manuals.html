Search by GC number or Manufacturer

Every gas appliance has a Gas Council (GC) number in order to identify that specific make and model. Your appliances GC number is found on the databadge, which is located on your appliance (Please note, not all appliances have their databadges visible).

If you can't find your GC number, don't worry! Our easy to find guide helps you locate your appliance's manual with ease. Just select your appliances make below and start typing the model number.
